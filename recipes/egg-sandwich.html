Egg Sandwich

Image of completed dish

An egg sandwich is one of the first meals most people will learn to make. Its fairly easy, delicious and low cost. To make it morer interesting, cheease, lettuce and tomato can be added.

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • cooking oil
  • 2 slices of bread
  • butter or margarine

Preparation

  1. Heat frying pan
  2. Add a small amount of oil to fully coat the pan
  3. Break eggs and pour in a bowl and whisk using fork until the mixture takes on the colour of the yoke
  4. Pour mixture into the pot and whisk
  5. Whisk for about 15 seconds or until the mixture is semi-solid
  6. Toast the breads to required taste (optional)
  7. Spread small amount of butter on both slices
  8. Scoop egg out of pot and placed between the breads
