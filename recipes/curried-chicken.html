Curried Chicken

Completed dish

Curried chicken is a delicious dish. It is easy to prepare, and fairly inexpensive. To ensure the dish is flavourful, it is recommended it is easoned and left to marinade overnight. Adding potatoes and carrots gives it a unique flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1 x whole chicken
  • 1 tbspn Curried powder
  • Black pepper
  • Sprinkle of Salt
  • 2 x large potatoes
  • 2 x Carrots
  • 2 x large Tomatoes

Steps

Remove the skin from the chicken, and wash with vinegar or lime.Sprinkle curry powder, black pepper, and salt onto chicken in a large container and stir to evenly coat the chicken. Cover and put in fridge overnight. Pour 1 tablespoon olive oil in a large heated frying pan. When oil is hot, pour chicken into pot and turn dowm to medium heat. Keep stirring until the chicken is brown. Turn the heat to a low setting, and add sliced carrots and tomatoes. Let simmer for 1 hour.
