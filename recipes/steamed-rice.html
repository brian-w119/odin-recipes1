Steamed Rice

Image of completed dish

Ingredients

  • 0.5 kg of white rice
  • Margarine
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Wash rice thoroughly
  2. Place rice in pot and fill with water just above the level of rice
  3. Turn heat on high and when it start to boil turn down low to simmer
  4. Add tablespoon of butt er and stir, then cover pot and let simmer for 15 minutes
  5. Use fork to test that the rest is soft
  6. If necessary, add a smal amount of water
  7. When rice is fluffy and soft, remove from heat and let cool
